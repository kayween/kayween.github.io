Let \\( \Av \\) be a \\( n \times n \\) square matrix.
We are interested in its square root.
\\[
    \Xv \Xv = \Av
\\]

Note that \\( \Xv \\) may not be diagonalizable.

\\(
    \Xv = (1, 1; 0, -1)
\\)

## A Different Square Root

Hermitian root

\\[
    \Xv^\top \Xv = \Av
\\]
